Tonkotsu Ramen

Description: bowl of ramen containing pork belly, ramen eggs, and vegetables garnished with spring onions

This recipe requires a lot of preparation, but the result will ruin restaurant ramen for you forever

Ingredience:

For the pork belly

  • 3 strips pork belly
  • 25ml soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp crispy chilli oil
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 10g sugar

for the broth

  • 1 chicken stock pot
  • 500ml boiling water
  • 1 tbsp miso paste
  • 4 spring onions
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 lump of ginger
  • ramen noodles

Everything else

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 heads of Pak Choi
  • kimchi

Method:

prepare the eggs

  1. bring pot to a boil
  2. add eggs, lower heat to a simmer, cover and cook for 7 minutes
  3. remove eggs from pot and place in ice bath to prevent them cooking further
  4. in a bowl, add 500ml water and 100ml soy sauce
  5. peel eggs and place in soy sauce solution
  6. chill in fridge for minimum 2 hours

Prepare the pork belly

  1. Place the pork belly in a vacuum seal bag
  2. season with soy sauce, sugar, chilli oil and sesame oil
  3. vacuum seal bag and cook sous vide at 74 degrees and cook for 6 hours

Prepare the broth

  1. add all ingredients into a pot
  2. boil for 2 hours
  3. strain out large vegetables

Put it all together!

  1. boil the noodles in a separate pot
  2. once cooked, strain the noodles and drench in cold water
  3. chop pak choi into strips, separating the leaves
  4. boil pak choi in broth for 3 minutes, adding leaves after that time and boiling for additional 1 minute
  5. place cold noodles in a bowl
  6. cover with broth
  7. chop pork belly into thin slices and place in bowl
  8. place pak choi in bowl
  9. cut eggs in half and place 2 halves in bowl
  10. garnish with kimchi
  11. Enjoy!
