Sourdough Boule

Description: A perfectly baked sourdough loaf

This recipe will require a sourdough starter, we won't be making one here but if you were lucky enough to survive lockdown maybe you have one around?

Ingredients:

  • 500g strong bread flour
  • 300ml water
  • 10g salt
  • 200g sourdough starter

Method:

  1. In a stand mixer, add the water and sourdough starter
  2. mix thoroughly
  3. add the flour and salt, mix until combined
  4. let rest for 30 mins to autolyse
  5. knead until smooth
  6. place dough in a bowl in a warm dry place, wait 30 mins
  7. periodically, every 30 mins, stretch the dough, be sure to not knock out as many air bubbles as possible
  8. after it's been folded at least six times, transfer it to a floured banetton
  9. allow the final prove to occur for at least an hour
  10. place in a preheated oven, inside a dutch oven, cutting some lines with a lame, spraying with water before covering
  11. bake for 30 mins covered, 20 uncovered
  12. let cool before cutting, this is important despite how delicious this is gonna smell
  13. enjoy with some salted butter or dipped in olive oil and balsamic vinegar
